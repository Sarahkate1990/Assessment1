My Interest in Information Technology


Since I was a kid I have always grown up with Information Technology, my father used to take me to computer shows with him and we would look at all the different computer parts.
My dad would tell me about them and taught me how they were used. This sparked a great interest and from there I began researching other areas, Web Development in particular.
At the age of 17 I left highschool and studied IT at TAFE NSW. I never got to finish any of the certificates, but that didn’t change my mind about IT.
Many years later, after a lot of up’s and down’s here I am at RMIT pursuing my goals and on the path to becoming a Website Developer.
