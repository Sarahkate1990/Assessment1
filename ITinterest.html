My Interest in Information Technology


Since I was a kid I have always grown up with Information Technology, my father used to take me to computer shows with him and we would look at all the different computer parts.
My dad would tell me about them and taught me how they were used. This sparked a great interest and from there I began researching other areas, Web Development in particular.
At the age of 17 I left highschool and studied IT at TAFE NSW. I never got to finish any of the certificates, but that didn’t change my mind about IT.
Many years later, after a lot of up’s and down’s here I am at RMIT pursuing my goals and on the path to becoming a Website Developer.


In 2007, I decided to study at TAFE instead of continuing on to year 11, and there I studied a Certificate III in Information Technology (applications) to explore the industry a little bit and get a bit more knowledge.
I really enjoyed this course, especially learning about websites. This sparked a great interest in web development when we were given an assignment to build a web page using only css/html. My webpage received 85% marks and this gave me a lot of confidence and thus started my passion for web development.


In 2011 I continued on to study a Certificate IV in Information Technology (websites), and my love for IT and Websites grew even more. I thought about studying for a Bachelor of IT but this would require a year 12 certificate and an ATAR ranking,
which both I did not have, so i enrolled into an open foundation course at The University of Newcastle, but unfortunately due to personal life issues and mental health I was unable to continue.
Since then, I have moved around a lot and had 2 kids, which put any career plans on hold for a few years. Luckily this year in 2020 I stumbled across an advertisement on social media for Open Universities so I decided to enquire about the Bachelor of IT.
This is when they introduced me to RMIT online foundation units, and I must say it has been the best thing that happen this year and I am so excited and proud to be on this journey, finally working towards my dream of studying for a Bachelor of IT.
